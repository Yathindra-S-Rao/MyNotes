About MyNotes

This application is web-based solution to create and store any information as notes. The application allows user to create their own profile by registering with email id. user can create, edit, archive or delete their notes. Since the app is web-based, user can check out their notes anywhere and anytime with any sort of devices such as Laptop, Desktops, Smartphones and Notebooks.
